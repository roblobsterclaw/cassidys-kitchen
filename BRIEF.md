## Brand Vibe
Classic American comfort food with personality. Hot griddle, fresh-off-the-flat-top, no-nonsense street food energy. Welcoming, hardworking, proud. Rustic, strong, straight to the point. Blue-collar, family-owned, local favorite — but polished enough to build a real brand.

**Brand Description:**
"Cassidy's Kitchen is a family-owned food truck serving up bold comfort food with a hot-off-the-griddle attitude. From stacked smash burgers to satisfying breakfast sandwiches, everything is made to feel familiar, filling, and full of flavor — simple food done right, with pride behind every order."

## Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Bold yellow (#FFD700ish) + black + white

- **Truck:** Yellow trailer with black diamond plate trim, string lights, fold-up serving awning
- **Feel:** Jersey Fresh, street food, family pride
